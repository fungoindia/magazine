AI-guided intuition

Pure mathematics involves the discovery of patterns between mathematical objects and using these connections to formulate conjectures. Mathematicians have deployed computers since the 1960s to help them uncover such patterns, but in this week’s issue, Alexander Davies and his colleagues demonstrate the use of machine learning for the process.
